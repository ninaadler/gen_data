  1. How would you interpret the beta coefficient from this regression?

The regression coefficient for rs1861 under the addititve model is 0.967 (p = 3.82e-79). The positive coefficient means that each additional copy of the C allele is associated with an increased chance of having the phenotype. The low p-value means that this association is statistically significant.
